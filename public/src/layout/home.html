Welcome

This is the online dictionary for the orcish language created by Matt Vancil

Main Features:

  • Word List
    • View all the words
    • Sort by Orcish, English or Part of Speech
    • Limit by Part of Speech
  • Adjectives
    • View both their feminine and masculine/neutral genders for all 5 cases
    • Every form combination
      • gender (feminine and masculine/neutral) and
      • case (nominative, genitive, dative, accusative and vocative)
  • Nouns
    • Every form combination
      • number (singular and plural) and
      • case (nominative, genitive, dative, accusative and vocative)
  • Verbs
    • Every form combination
      • number (singular and plural),
      • person (1st, 2nd and 3rd),
      • voice (active and passive), and
      • tense (past, present, future, past perfect and future perfect)
    • Imperative form
    • Gerund form (verb as noun e.g. killing is fun)
    • Participle form (verb as adjective e.g. the lying human)
    • Agent form (person who performs the verb's action e.g. "to bake" becomes "baker")
    • Feminine, masculine and dishonorable agent forms
  • Sentences
    • View by category

Searching:

  • Search using orcish
    • Find all words who's orcish match
    • Find all word cases that match
    • Get information about the match type e.g. searching for ashkagek tells us its the past active 3rd person plural form of the word ka
    • Find matches that use suffixes and prefixes e.g. searching for keheldjart tells us its the imperative singular form of the word heldja with the ke- prefix
    • Find matches where the prefix attaches to the head of a verb e.g. searching for arkeheldjag tells us its the future active 1st person singular form of the word heldja with the ke- prefix
  • Search using english
    • Searches using english, extra info, coined by and named after fields

Admin Features:

  • Autofill button to automatically generate cases/conjugations for nouns, verbs and adjectives
  • Bulk Add page for adding multiple words at once
  • Help buttons on New Word, Edit Word and Bulk Add pages
  • Statistics page
